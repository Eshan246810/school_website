Overview

About twelve years ago, Sanskriti Educational Society was founded by a group of like-minded individuals to fulfil their objective, they entered into an agreement with the Delhi Public School Society, which is known worldwide for its 200+ schools rendering quality education to lakhs of students. Thus, was born the first Delhi Public School at Vadodara. The DPS Society is a non-profit, non-proprietary, private, educational organization. This Global Network of over 200 English medium, co-educational, secular schools provide education from Pre-Nursery/Nursery to Class XII.
